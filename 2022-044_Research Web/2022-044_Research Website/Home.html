INTRODUCTION

Tamil Correcto+ is an advanced application developed as part of our research project to assist Tamil language users with spell correction, grammar correction, speech-to-text conversion, and text summarization. This application is especially valuable for students and educational institutions that have shifted to online and hybrid learning due to the COVID-19 pandemic. By providing accurate spell and grammar corrections, Tamil Correcto+ enhances the quality of written content. The speech-to-text feature allows users to convert spoken Tamil into text efficiently, saving time and minimizing distractions. Additionally, the text summarization tool condenses lengthy texts, retaining only the essential information for easier comprehension. Tamil Correcto+ aims to simplify the use of the Tamil language in digital interactions, significantly improving the writing, speaking, and reading experience while saving time and effort, thereby enhancing overall productivity.

E-LEARNING TRENDS TO WATCH-OUT

The COVID-19 pandemic significantly impacted educational institutions, drastically altering teaching and testing methods in schools and colleges. However, this gap was swiftly bridged by applications like Tamil Correcto+, which facilitate online learning for Tamil language users. As educational institutions rapidly transitioned to online teaching and learning, Tamil Correcto+ became essential. With people spending more time at home than ever before, tools like Tamil Correcto+ are crucial for maintaining educational continuity, enhancing the quality of Tamil language education through features like spell correction, grammar correction, speech-to-text conversion, and text summarization.
