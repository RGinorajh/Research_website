Our PROJECT SCOPE

Literature Survey & Research Gap

This literature review explores the importance of language-specific NLP tools for languages like Tamil, which have distinct characteristics compared to English. Spell-checking is a critical aspect in language processing, especially in Tamil, where word forms exhibit variations based on context. Context-aware spell-checking is crucial for accurate corrections due to the intricate morphology of Tamil words. Identifying grammatical errors is another essential aspect contributing to enhanced language comprehension. Languages divergent from the Indo-European paradigm, such as Tamil, introduce complexities to NLP tasks due to agglutination and context-dependent word formations. This underscores the need for NLP tools sensitive to diverse cultural and linguistic nuances. Research on real-time speech-to-text transcription has provided valuable insights into prevalent challenges and breakthroughs, but these studies predominantly focus on widely spoken languages. Adapting these methodologies to the phonetic intricacies of Tamil speech requires dedicated research endeavors. Addressing the dearth of digital resources for languages like Tamil is imperative, as specialized methodologies are indispensable given the relative scarcity of digital materials compared to more widely spoken languages. The influence of cultural and contextual factors on language comprehension is also crucial. Language processing tools should extend beyond mere error detection and incorporate cultural sensitivity, particularly in Tamil, where linguistic and cultural intricacies intertwine. The research aims to adopt a holistic approach, developing a language processing tool that not only addresses linguistic challenges but also resonates with and embraces the cultural richness of the Tamil language.

Research Problem

The scarcity of language-specific Natural Language Processing (NLP) tools for Tamil poses significant challenges in accurate language processing. Tamil's unique morphological structure, context-dependent word formations, and cultural nuances necessitate advanced spell-checking, grammatical error identification, and summarization systems. Current NLP research predominantly focuses on widely spoken languages, leaving Tamil underrepresented. Consequently, there is an urgent need for developing context-aware, culturally sensitive NLP tools tailored to Tamil. This research aims to bridge this gap by creating comprehensive language processing solutions, including summarization tools, that enhance accuracy and embrace the linguistic and cultural richness of the Tamil language.

Research Objectives

Implement a spell-checker with context-aware correction suggestions.
Implement an online grammar-checker with context-aware correction suggestions.
Build a context-preserving online summarizer.
Create a real-time Tamil speech-to-text transcription with enhanced accuracy.


Methodology

The research methodology consists of many key processes that must be completed in a methodical manner to achieve the given objectives. During the first phase, extensive data is gathered on how the Tamil language is utilized in a variety of situations and digital media. Following that, preprocessing and feature extraction are critical steps in increasing the quality of the obtained text and audio data. Following that, spell-checking and grammar-correction options were introduced. This necessitated developing algorithms capable of dealing with the Tamil language's complicated morphology and shifting conditions. Developing a contextual text summarizing mechanism at the same time necessitates approaches that can comprehend the complicated structure of Tamil sentences and provide a brief yet informative summary. The last stage is to integrate all these components into a unified web-based platform. This platform will be thoroughly tested for accuracy, usability, and user happiness, and it will be upgraded continuously depending on user feedback and evolving linguistic trends. In summary, this study technique provides a comprehensive and systematic approach to improving Tamil language processing on digital platforms.
